* Players start with a deck containing - 

4 bullets ( SALVAGE ) 
4 tinned ham ( SALVAGE )
1 character special ability ( PLAYER )
1 mild injury ( CATASTROPHE )

* A game day consists of 5 phases: 

1. WAKE UP
2. PLAN
3. EXPLORE
4. HUNGER
5. REST

1. WAKE UP
* Players draw 5 cards from their deck. 

2. PLAN
* Players may exchange Salvage Cards with one another. Trades must be 1-for-1. Survivor Cards and Injury Cards may not be traded. 

3. EXPLORE
* In the explore phase, all players either: 
    - Select an unexplored location
    OR 
    - Select an explored location.
